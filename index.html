Khawaja Ajmeer Steel Industries Limited, a key concern of KSRM Group, is located in Madambibirhat, Chattogram. Its production capacity of 5000 metric tons per year encompasses a Rolling unit and a Foundry Works Unit. This facility, comprising three factory sheds, not only caters to commercial demands but also serves as a backup for other production units within the KSRM Group. The Rolling unit specializes in manufacturing various sizes of Plain and Deformed Bars, with its Plain Bars notably sought after in industrial applications such as nail and screw production. Additionally, the factory produces rolls and spare parts essential for the group's manual mills.
